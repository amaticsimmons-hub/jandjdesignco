Services & Pricing

Clear 3-tier packages across website design, logo design, and website hosting management—so you can choose support that fits where your business is right now.

Website Design

Custom-designed websites built on user-friendly platforms like Wix and AWS-backed hosting. Each tier includes mobile-responsive layouts, on-brand visuals, and a focus on clarity and conversion.

Starter – $450

Ideal for new businesses or personal brands that need a clean, professional online home quickly. Includes a simple layout, core sections (about, services, contact), and basic launch support.

Growth – $750

Designed for growing brands ready to showcase more services, portfolio items, or booking links. Includes multi-page structure, guided content flow, and light optimization for user experience.

Premium – $1250

Best for established businesses needing a polished, strategic website experience. Includes advanced layouts, more pages or sections, blog/resource setup, and launch walkthrough.

Logo Design

Brand marks that match your vision—paired with the right colors and typography so your visuals feel consistent everywhere you show up.

Essential – $175

A refresh for brands that already have an idea or existing logo. Includes one polished concept, font and color refinement, and delivery in high-resolution files for print and digital use.

Standard – $325

Ideal for new brands that want options. Includes multiple logo concepts, main and secondary logo variations, basic brand usage guidance, and color/black-and-white versions.

Premium – $550

A full visual identity experience. Includes logo suite (primary, secondary, submark), curated color palette, typography pairings, social media-ready graphics, and a mini brand guide PDF.

Hosting Management

Ongoing care for your live website. We monitor, update, and support your site so it stays secure, functional, and aligned with your current offers and content.

Care – $55/mo

Great for lean sites that need eyes on uptime and basic upkeep. Includes domain and hosting monitoring, simple platform updates, and monthly health checks.

Managed – $95/mo

For active businesses that make occasional updates. Includes everything in Care plus a set number of content edits each month and priority email support for minor changes or fixes.

Pro – $155/mo

A “done-for-you” management option. Includes frequent edits, performance checks, tech integration support (forms, tools, booking), and strategy-focused check-ins.

Meet Amati Simmons

Amati Simmons is the lead designer and founder of J&J Design Co., based in Toledo, Ohio. With 10+ years of experience in graphic design and digital media, Amati specializes in creating visuals and web experiences that feel polished, modern, and strategic.

As a Canva, Wix, and AWS-certified partner, Amati blends user-friendly platforms with reliable tech infrastructure—making it easier for small business owners and entrepreneurs to maintain a professional online presence without feeling overwhelmed.
